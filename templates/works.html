02 : Works

Penetration Tester A Penetration Tester, also known as an Ethical Hacker, assesses the security of computer systems and networks by simulating attacks to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses that could be exploited by malicious hackers. Penetration Testers work to find weaknesses in security systems before illegal hackers can exploit them. They are responsible for testing and identifying security vulnerabilities in software, hardware, and networks.

001/006

Responsibilities

Conducting both manual and automated penetration tests on computer systems and networks. Identifying weaknesses and vulnerabilities in networks and systems. Collaborating with IT and security teams to develop and implement effective security measures. Preparing detailed reports of vulnerabilities found and providing recommendations for remediation.
